Group Processes

Overall the team performed extremely well in assignment 2. We received constructive feedback outlining the changes we needed to make. These included changes to the assignment, noted below, and other minor team specific issues.

Changes made this assignment relative to the previous is that we are adding another major component to our project. Initial project plan was to have an arduino board configured to show the temperature and humidity of the surroundings which can later be taken to school and demonstrated to attract more students into the field of information technology.

After the feedback received from assignment 2, we thought it was best to add another component which is a chatbot in partnership with the arduino board. These two paired together will assist its user to gain critical weather information of both the surroundings and the chatbot (if connected to the internet) would provide other weather related information to the user.

 Group Reflections

Alessio's reflection

This time around communication was much easier as we have all gotten to know each other, similar to the previous task however one issue we ran into was regarding platforms.

We initially opted to use a website called Figma to design the mockup designs however this tool proved to be counter intuitive and not alligned with what we needed.

We then began work on an alternative called gomockingbird which proved to be much easier to work with. Like the previous project we found that sticking to basics proved to be what we needed to stay on track with the same tools used again to complete this project.

In terms of working with the group I again found Anton and Vignesh to be great team members who are both easygoing and were dedicated to making sure every aspect was covered and that we saw this through.

Similar to other work we started by breaking apart the assignment into all the necessary components and then assigned roles to each other enabling us to quickly assemble all content before I took everything and published the website.

The main challenge throughout the assignment was initially regular communication to establish the direction of the project however after the first  2 Skype calls this was quickly resolved. All up I have enjoyed working with Anton and Vignesh as well as this entire course with RMIT.

Overall Group Reflection:

Early on we found it difficult on settling for a project idea that we could create a presentation around and that we would use to build on the Arduino system we originally hoped to use. 

After a few Skype calls and talking with the group we decided that a chatbot would be a nice addition to the board and could add an extra layer of functionality which could be expanded upon in many different ways. 

Once we decided we took a similar approach to our last project and started breaking down the document into all the various sub-headings assigning roles along the way to each other to get the job done. 

As we learnt from previous assignments keeping things as simple and streamlined as possible was critical along with starting earlier to avoid issues that may set us back.

Overall we felt like we achieved this and building on our experiences were able to swiftly complete the task at hand and maintain constant communication throughout.

The course has really helped us to be able to go into a random group and get to know each other well to come together and coordinate a successful project.
