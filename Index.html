Temptation Station - Your source for temperature information



A project by AAV (Advanced Academy of Virtual Assistants)

RMIT Assignment 3-5 Task: Our Project

What is Temptation station?

Temptation station is a integrated all-in-one solution designed to provide you with temperature and weather information directly in a chatbot interface powered by an Arduino appliance to collect data such as humidity, temperature and more.
